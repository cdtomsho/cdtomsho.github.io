Welcome to the Wall

After being included in the 2020 Summer Olympics, rock climbing has sky-rocketed in popularity, becoming one of the fastest growing outdoor sports in the world! Thanks to legends like Alex Honnold, Janja Garnbret, and Adam Ondra, climbing has hit the mainstream, and each of their climbing styles showcase how individualized any climber's experience can be.

Rock climbing combines physical strength, mental focus, and a deep connection with the outdoors. Whether you're drawn to the gym or natural rock (the crag), this guide will help you understand the basics and get started with confidence!

This site covers the main types of climbing, the essential gear you'll need, and what to expect as a beginner. No experience required! Just curiosity & a willingness to try something new.
